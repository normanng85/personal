About Me

Strategic healthcare executive with 10+ years of progressive leadership experience in quality improvement, patient safety, hospital operations, and organizational transformation.

My work is driven by a commitment to building systems that protect patients and empower frontline teams. I have led quality and safety programs across behavioral health, academic psychiatry, and community-based settings — translating evidence-based frameworks into measurable, lasting improvements.

Grounded in both clinical practice and executive leadership, I bring a combination of hands-on experience in addiction treatment, rigorous quality science (Lean Six Sigma, HRO, Just Culture), and the stakeholder partnership skills to drive change at scale.

Currently based in Portland, Maine, where I continue to consult with healthcare organizations committed to operational excellence and patient-centered care.
